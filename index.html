Sounds in the Woods, by Jack Kerouac

25 words are chosen randomly in the poem Sounds in the Woods by Jack Kerouac and mashed together. The original text is already a mix of non-sensical passages and various onomatopoeia meant to conjure buzzing insects and other forest noises. Mashing this cryptic text is perhaps more like taking small snapshots of a bigger landscape than cutting a text apart and discovering new meanings in its pieces.
